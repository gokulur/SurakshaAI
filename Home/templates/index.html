Suraksha — Check Suspicious Links Instantly with AI

Suraksha helps you verify suspicious links before clicking — using advanced AI models trained to detect phishing, scams, and malicious websites.

  • Submit any link to instantly check if it's safe or a potential threat.
  • AI-powered analysis based on URL behavior, structure, and threat databases.
  • Clear, fast results: Safe, Suspicious, or Phishing — with explanations.
  • No installation or account needed — just paste and scan.
  • Built to protect individuals and businesses from online fraud.

Whether it’s a link from email, WhatsApp, or social media, Suraksha gives you instant peace of mind before you open it.

Our vision is clear: by 2027, we aim to drastically reduce phishing attacks by empowering users with AI-powered threat detection.

Suraksha is simple, fast, and effective — because security should be accessible to everyone.
